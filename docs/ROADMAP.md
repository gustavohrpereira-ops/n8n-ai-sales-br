## English Version

This document outlines the development plan for the `n8n-ai-sales-br` ecosystem and related products.

### ✅ Completed (Phase 1)

#### 1. 10 n8n Workflows (This Repository)
- Complete sales cycle automation for the collective purchasing (consórcio) market.
- Integration with Claude 3.5 Sonnet for scoring, messaging, and proposals.
- **Status:** Live and Open Source.

#### 2. Financial Dashboard for Brokers (MVP)
- SaaS platform with financial analysis via Claude.
- **Status:** MVP Finalized.

### 🚧 In Development (Phase 2)

#### 1. Financial Dashboard SaaS – Expansion and Scale
- **Focus on 10,000+ Advisors:** Infrastructure optimization to support massive data volumes.
- **Autopilot Mode:** Implementation of 100% autonomous flows for repetitive back-office tasks.
- **Major Players:** Homologation and integration for Ademicon, Itaú, Porto Seguro, and other strategic partners.
- Deep integration with Pipedrive and Hubspot.

#### 2. 5 Claude-Powered Landing Pages
- Lead capture pages with integrated intelligent chatbots.
- Conversion optimization (CRO) based on real sales data.

### 📅 Future Vision (Phase 3)

#### 1. IA Multi-Agent for Complex Sales
- Collaboration between different AI "personas" for credit analysis and closing.
- Focus on consultative serving and high-value delivery to clients.

#### 2. Market Expansion
- Adaptation of workflows for Real Estate, Insurance, and Large-Scale Consórcios across Latin America.
- Expansion to all companies in the sector with a larger number of advisors.
